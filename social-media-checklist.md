# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (For College Students)

## ✅ Do's
- ✔️ Think before you post — consider how it may affect your future.
- ✔️ Protect your privacy — use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.
- ✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- ✔️ Be respectful and positive in comments and interactions.
- ✔️ Use social media for learning, networking, and personal growth.
- ✔️ Manage your time — set limits to avoid distraction from studies.

## ❌ Don'ts
- ❌ Don't share personal or sensitive information publicly (address, phone, etc.).
- ❌ Don't engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or hate speech.
- ❌ Don't post inappropriate or offensive content.
- ❌ Don't believe everything you see — avoid fake news and rumors.
- ❌ Don't overshare your daily life or location in real-time.
- ❌ Don't let social media affect your mental health — avoid unhealthy comparisons.
